Tabelas IMC
Results less than 16 — severe thinness;
Results between 16 and 16.9 — moderate thinness;
Results between 17 and 18.5 — mild thinness;
Results between 18.6 and 24.9 — ideal weight;
Results between 25 and 29.9 — overweight;
Results between 30 and 34.9 — grade I obesity;
Results between 35 and 39.9 — grade II obesity;
Results greater than 40 — grade III obesity.
